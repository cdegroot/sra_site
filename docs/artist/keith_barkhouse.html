Keith Barkhouse

Keith Barkhouse is an artist who lives in Eugenia, Ontario but originates from Antigonish, Nova Scotia.  Keith lives with his other half, three dogs, Ernie, Marly & Hilton and his three cats, Victoria, Ireland and Giles.  He is always producing so be sure to stay connected with him on his Society6 page.  Keith's art has been described by his friend Lisa as pseudo nouveau pop art with japenese infuleuence with which Keith agrees!
