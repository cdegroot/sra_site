Robert Pointer

Robert Pointer is a Canadian artist and educator working in central Ontario. His  artwork has been in exhibition since the 1970's. He primarily creates intimate paintings in acrylic on canvas. He retired in 2014 after teaching art and music at the middle school level for over 25 years. He continues to teach art with Wildwood Art Adventures.
