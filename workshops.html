Workshop
Painting Trees with Peter J. Reid

Dates:
November 22nd, 2017 and
November 29th, 2017

Time:  
9:00 am to 3:00 pm

Materials Required:
Please bring a reference photo, a canvas and paints and brushes.  

Format:
Instruction in the morning; work on your own project in the afternoon. Please note there is an instructional booklet used during class time which can be purchased for you to keep at a cost of $5.00

Registration:
Email splitrailartists@gmail.com 
or by phone at 519-924-2734

Cost:
$90 for members for both days
$100 for non-members for both days.

Cash or Cheque payable to Split Rail Artists

Location:  
Flesherton Legion
14 Elizabeth Street

Learn essential techniques for painting trees in acrylic. Capture the qualities of every season in forestscapes.

"If you've had problems painting trees that do not look like trees then sign up.  You'll become a full fledged arborist.....well maybe not but people will now not ask you "what's that thing with a stick?" ~ Peter J. Reid
